About

Compare-It lets you easily compare items across different categories to help you identify the most preferred choice. Simply create a category, add your items, and let Compare-It guide your decisions.

How It Works

The app pairs your items for head-to-head comparisons, ensuring each item is matched against every other item only once. Your selections determine which items come out on top.
